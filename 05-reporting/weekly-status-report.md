# Weekly Status Report

Use this report to give leadership, sponsors, stakeholders, and workstream owners a clear update on program progress.

The weekly status report should answer five questions:

1. Are we on track?
2. What was completed?
3. What is coming next?
4. What is at risk?
5. What decisions or support are needed?

## Executive Summary

Write 3 to 5 sentences summarizing the current state of the program.

Include:

- Current progress
- Major wins
- Key risks
- Decisions needed
- What happens next

Example:

The program remains on track for the planned launch date. This week, the team completed stakeholder review, finalized the draft workflow, and confirmed training needs. One dependency remains open with Finance, but mitigation steps are in place. Next week, the team will finalize training materials, confirm the launch communication, and review readiness with leadership.
